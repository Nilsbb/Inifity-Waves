Infinity Waves

Smell the sea air

Infinity Waves is a sea waves simulator. From a simple file audio, Howler, jQuery and HTML5, the sound is modulated with random fadein and fadeout sequences.

Message in the bottle

It's time to book a holiday. Maybe somewhere across the seas. Somewhere I haven't been before. I want to go somewhere. Just anywhere I just don't care.
